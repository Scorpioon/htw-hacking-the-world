About HTW

A platform built to detect and interpret contemporary signals.

HTW brings together strategic analysis, culture, technology and visual systems to read what is happening now and what may come next.

Francesca Tur

Communication, research and teaching converge in a transversal reading of the present. The platform is rooted in a broad contextual understanding of media, symbols and behaviour.

Berta Segura

Strategic analysis, futures and generation-focused observation provide the operational structure that turns references into frameworks and insights into tools.

Visual principle

The HTW identity is structured around a simple thesis: the pixel is the minimum unit of analysis. The visual system uses grid, iconography and image disruption as a formal equivalent of how the platform reads complexity.
